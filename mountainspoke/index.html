Let's go for a ride!

Whether you are an experienced rider, or are looking for knowledgable teachers to show you the ropes, we have something for everyone. Since 1996, we've helped over 10,000 people from around the world get more out of life through heart-pounding, fist-pumping, soul-fulfilling mountain bike adventures in some of the most spectacular scenery in Montana.

We are passionate about mountain biking, and our goal is to provide the best bikes, parts, repair service, and biking tours in the West.

Does your bike need a tune-up?

Whether it’s a small brake adjustment or a complete bike overhaul, we’re happy to help. Our highly experienced staff is here to answer any questions you may have and will help get you and your bike back on the trail as quickly as possible.
